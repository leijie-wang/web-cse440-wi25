## Overview

You are to use insights from your design research to inform a process of defining a design.
You will first identify a set of key tasks within the space of your problem.
Then you will propose multiple design alternatives, each taking a distinct approach to supporting these tasks.
Finally, you will choose among these alternatives and use two key tasks to further detail your design and its context.
